My diverse cultural background, shaped by living in Sweden, Ukraine, Ireland, and the UK, has highlighted the significance of embracing various cultures. I find immense value in understanding people's backgrounds and the rich tapestry of experiences that shape them. Recognizing the importance of diverse perspectives has become a key element in my approach to succeeding in any business industry. I am currently a Junior at the Gabelli School of Business at Fordham University, completing a Bachelor's degree in Global Business with a concentration in Design Media and Technology and a minor in New Media and Digital Design.

My college journey has ignited my fascination with digital design across various mediums, leading me to explore industries like VFX, Animation, Web Development, and Video Game Development. In my spare time, I'm an avid enthusiast of animations and VFX films, constantly amazed by the incredible possibilities digital design can unlock when paired with magical and compelling narratives. My leadership journey started as a secretary and eventually led me to serve as the President of the Marketing Club for two semesters. This experience highlighted the importance of effective communication in any form, be it discussions, social media posts, or even video games. I'm deeply passionate about harnessing the power of digital design to create effective means of communication that precisely address consumer needs. My experience with the Marketing Club has underscored the significance of collaborative work settings, fueling my desire for a dynamic learning environment where all my colleagues are my mentors.

I currently am building my technical skills in learning programming languages such as HTML, CSS, and Javascript and software such as Maya, Houdini, and Blender.
